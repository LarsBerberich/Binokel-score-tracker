About Binokel

Binokel (or Binocle) is a traditional Swabian card game that originated from the Württemberg region and is traditionally played with a Württemberg deck.

The game requires strategic thinking and a good memory. The specialized Binokel cards are widely available.

Basic Rules:
  • Typically played with 3 or 4 players
  • The deck consists of 48 cards in the suits of Hearts, Diamonds (Schell), Clubs (Eichel), and Spades (Blatt)
  • Cards are dealt counterclockwise
  • Players bid for the right to choose trump and pick up additional cards
  • Points are earned through melding combinations and taking tricks
  • The first player to reach 1000 points wins a round
